Follow-up & Prioritization Strategy

A systematic outreach sequence is what separates research from BD. Here is the exact playbook.

7-Day Outreach Sequence — Per Company

0

Day 0 — Research & Warm Up

Find the Partnerships Manager or BD Manager (not the CEO) on LinkedIn. Follow the company. Like or comment genuinely on 1–2 of their recent posts. This seeds name recognition before first contact and lifts reply rates significantly.

1

Day 1 — Email + LinkedIn Request Simultaneously

Send the cold email and LinkedIn connection request on the same day. Two channels, same message. This signals genuine intent — not spam. Different people respond to different channels; doubling up is professional.

2

Day 4 — Soft Follow-Up Email

Reply to your own email thread — don't start a new one. Keep it to 2 sentences: "Bumping this in case it got buried. Happy to send a 1-pager if useful." No pressure. Log this as a separate outreach attempt with a screenshot.

3

Day 7 — LinkedIn DM (If Connected)

If the connection was accepted, send the DM template. Reference the email casually: "Also dropped an email earlier this week — would love your team's take." If not yet connected, send an InMail with the connection note template.

4

Day 14 — Final Attempt with New Value

One last email with a fresh angle — a user stat, a new feature, or a relevant industry news hook. Then move on. Log it, update the tracker, and shift energy to the next company. Persistence is professional; desperation is not.
